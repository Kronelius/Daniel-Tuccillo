Questions

The things people ask first

Do I have to replace my current software?

No. Plenty of gigs are just connecting tools you already pay for so they actually talk to each other. If you are overpaying, I'll show you where — but the call is always yours.

What does a project cost?

It depends on scope, which is exactly why the audit comes first. You get a fixed price before any work starts — no open-ended hourly surprises. Small automations often pay for themselves in the first month.

Is the AI going to spam my customers?

Never. I build TCPA-compliant, human-first systems. Automation handles speed and admin; your team keeps the relationship. No "AI slop," no risky mass-texting.

Do you only work with Seattle businesses?

I'm based in the Greater Seattle Area but work with home service companies remotely across the U.S.

What happens after the project ends?

You own the systems and get documentation plus team training. I offer ongoing support if you want it, but you're never locked in.
