About

The person behind the work

I'm Daniel Tuccillo. Before I wrote a line of automation, I spent 11+ years managing and selling inside service-driven businesses — long enough to feel every missed call, every bit of double-entry, and every software bill that crept up another notch each quarter. I was even a licensed real estate broker here in Washington. So when I build for a home service company, I'm building from inside the operator's chair, not guessing from a spec sheet.

These days I do two things. I build practical automation and custom apps for home service businesses — plumbers, HVAC, cleaners, landscapers — and I ship my own products: LeadStart AI and SaaSassins on the business side, GLD and ReciPal for the fun of it.

I care about work that's honest and human. No spammy mass-texting, no "AI slop," nothing that puts you on the wrong side of TCPA. Automation should handle the busywork so your team can handle the relationship. I'm based in the Greater Seattle area and work with service businesses remotely across the U.S.
